Information & facts

Planned Parenthood - Sexual Health Topics and Facts
Comprehensive and research based facts on a variety of topics including abortion, contraception, sexual orientation, and sexually transmitted infections
Sex & U
Website by the Society of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists of Canada (SOGC), Canada’s leading authority on sexual and reproductive health.
Guerilla Sex Ed
Resource for comprehensive sex ed geared towards people who seeking alternatives to school-based, abstinence-only sex ed. Includes information on advocacy and policy as well. 
Sex, etc.
Site for teens featuring articles written by teens. Covers a broad range of sex ed topics and includes polls, tools to find local clinics and take political action, and a blog. 
The Ultimate Guide to Sex and Disability
A complete sex guide for people who live with disabilities, pain, illness, or chronic conditions.
Our Bodies, Our Selves
Excerpts from books and web content by Our Bodies Ourselves (OBOS), a collective of women originally formed in 1969 that has been continually publishing books providing groundbreaking access to information on womens’ sexual health.
Ace: What Asexuality Reveals about Desire, Society, and the Meaning of Sex
A book about asexuality and what the asexual perspective can teach us all about desire and identity.

Advice & personal Experiences

Scarleteen
Site for teens covering variety of topics including bodies, gender, sex, relationships, abuse, disability, and more. Features an advice section and a message board. 
Go Ask Alice!
Anonymous Q&A site run by a team of health professionals from Columbia University with a database of questions on topics ranging from genital wonderings, orgasms, menstruation, and more. 
BISH UK 
A British site with inclusive and humorous information on sex, love, bodies, porn, and more. 
Rosie Respect
Australian site providing information for teen girls covering the body and mind, career and finances, relationships, and human rights. Relationships section includes many articles on sex, gender, and love. 
@the.vulva.gallery
Illustrations of female genitalia celebrating diversity of anatomy and providing educational information and personal anecdotes in captions. 
Hey Dr Sue
Humorous and fact-based Q&A blog run by sexuality educator Susan Milstein, PhD. 
QueerTips
Queer sex ed blog run by the Planned Parenthood of the Southern Finger Lakes with an archive of articles spanning variety of topics. 
Them
News and commentary through an LBTQ+ lens
